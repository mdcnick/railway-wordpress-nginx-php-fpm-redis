## Starter questions

- What is the main goal of this project?
- Who is the project for?
- What should the CEO care about most?
- What should the COO enforce?
- What should the CMO care about?
- Is there a CTO or technical decision-maker role?
- Should the project optimize for speed, quality, simplicity, automation, or revenue first?
